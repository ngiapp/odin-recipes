Spaghetti

Description

Lasagna is a tasty dish from Italy consisting of using different types of pasta and sauces.

There are many variations of the dishes such as Carbonara, Aglio Olio. It is usually finished off with cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Truffle
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  1. Boil sufficient water
  2. Add salt to water before adding pasta in
  3. Add pasta to water
  4. Cook till Al Dente
